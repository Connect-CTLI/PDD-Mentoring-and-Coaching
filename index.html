🎯 GROW Coaching Planner

David, this planner is designed specifically for you.

As you step into your new role as LMS Administrator, the GROW model provides a structured space for you to think deeply about your own development — not just what you need to learn, but why it matters and how you will get there. Completing each section honestly will help you and your coach focus your sessions on what will make the biggest difference to your confidence and performance. Your reflections are saved privately and form part of your coaching record. Click each heading below to open that section.
▶ 🎯 GOAL — What do you want to achieve?
David, take a moment to think about what success looks like for you in this role. Defining your goals clearly will give your coaching a focused direction and help you measure your own growth. Being specific here will make every coaching session more purposeful.

Consider:
  • Which LMS competencies require the most development?
  • What would successful performance as LMS Administrator look like?
  • What would you like to achieve by 30 June 2026?
  • Which responsibilities do you want to perform independently?
▶ 🔍 REALITY — Where are you now?
David, an honest reflection on where you are right now is one of the most valuable things you can do. It builds self-awareness, helps your coach understand your starting point, and ensures that your development plan is grounded in reality rather than assumption.

Consider:
  • What ICT and LMS experience do you already possess?
  • Which LMS tasks can you currently perform confidently?
  • Which tasks currently require guidance or support?
  • What development needs emerged from your self-assessment?
▶ 💡 OPTIONS — How can you develop?
David, this is your opportunity to think creatively about how you will grow. The more options you identify, the more agency you have over your own development. Your coach can open doors, but you are the one who walks through them.

Consider:
  • What support can your coach and colleagues provide?
  • Which practical workplace tasks will help build competence?
  • What resources, manuals or guides should you use?
  • Who could you observe or work alongside?
  • What opportunities exist for practice and feedback?
▶ 🚀 WAY FORWARD — What actions will you take?
David, this is where your plan becomes real. Committing to specific actions — with timelines and evidence — transforms reflection into progress. Your coach will use what you write here to support and hold you accountable between sessions.

Consider:
  • What specific actions will be completed before the next session?
  • What evidence will demonstrate progress?
  • What support is required from your coach?
  • What timelines will be followed?
  • How will success be measured at the end of the programme?
