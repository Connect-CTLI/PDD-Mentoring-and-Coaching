Welcome

This digital coaching hub has been developed to support a planned coaching and mentoring intervention within the Programme Design and Development Unit of CTLI.

Purpose of the Coaching Programme
The coaching programme has been designed to support workplace learning, knowledge transfer and role transition.

David Oliphant will be coached in preparation for assuming LMS Administration responsibilities within Connect@CTLI.

Bomikazi Majikijela will be coached in preparation for assuming Office Management responsibilities within the Programme Design and Development Unit.

🎯 GROW Coaching Planner
The GROW coaching model will guide your development as you transition into the LMS Administrator role. Use the development needs identified through your Skills Gap Analysis to inform your reflections and action planning.
▶ 🎯 GOAL
What do you want to achieve?

Consider:
  • Which LMS competencies require the most development?
  • What would successful performance as LMS Administrator look like?
  • What would you like to achieve by 30 June?
  • Which responsibilities do you want to perform independently?
▶ 🔍 REALITY
Where are you now?

Consider:
  • What ICT and Moodle experience do you already possess?
  • Which LMS tasks can you perform confidently?
  • Which tasks currently require support?
  • What development needs emerged from your assessment?
▶ 💡 OPTIONS
How can you develop?

Consider:
  • What support can your coach provide?
  • Which practical workplace tasks will help build competence?
  • What resources, manuals or guides should you use?
  • Who could you observe or work alongside?
  • What opportunities exist for practice and feedback?
▶ 🚀 WAY FORWARD
What actions will you take?

Consider:
  • What actions will be completed before the next coaching session?
  • What evidence will demonstrate progress?
  • What support is required?
  • What timelines will be followed?
  • How will success be measured?
