Welcome

This digital coaching hub has been developed to support a planned coaching and mentoring intervention within the Programme Design and Development Unit of CTLI.


The platform supports two staff members who are transitioning into new roles and provides digital tools aligned to the GROW coaching model.

🎯 Goal

Clarify developmental goals and expected outcomes for each coachee.

🔍 Reality

Assess current levels of knowledge, confidence and competence.

💡 Options

Explore strategies, resources and workplace learning opportunities.

🚀 Way Forward

Agree on practical actions and monitor progress over time.

Purpose of the Coaching Programme
The coaching programme has been designed to support workplace learning, knowledge transfer and role transition. David Oliphant will be coached in preparation for assuming LMS Administration responsibilities within Connect@CTLI. Bomikazi Majikijela will be coached in preparation for assuming Office Management responsibilities within the Programme Design and Development Unit. The programme combines workplace learning, reflective practice, coaching conversations and competency development.
