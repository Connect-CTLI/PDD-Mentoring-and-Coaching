🎯 GROW Coaching Planner
The GROW model will be used to support your transition into the role of LMS Administrator. Reflect on the development needs identified through your skills gap analysis and use the guiding questions below to help formulate your responses. Consider your responsibilities relating to: • User Administration • Enrolment Management • Reporting • Calendar Management • Basic Moodle Course Development • User Support

Consider the competencies you need to develop to perform successfully as LMS Administrator. Guiding questions: • What knowledge and skills do you need to develop? • What would successful performance in the role look like? • Which competency areas are your highest priority? • What would you like to achieve by the end of the coaching programme?



Reflect honestly on your current level of competence. Guiding questions: • Which LMS administration tasks are you already comfortable performing? • What previous experience do you have with Moodle or learning management systems? • Which tasks do you currently find challenging? • What knowledge gaps have been identified through the skills assessment?



Consider possible development opportunities. Guiding questions: • What support can your coach provide? • Which workplace activities could help you gain experience? • What resources, manuals or guides could support your learning? • Which practical tasks could help strengthen your competence? • Who could you observe or work alongside?



Identify specific actions that will be completed before the next coaching session. Guiding questions: • What will you do first? • What evidence will demonstrate progress? • What support do you require? • When will the actions be completed? • How will success be measured?
